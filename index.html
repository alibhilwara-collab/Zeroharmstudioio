“Tool Talks Break. Safety Circuits Must Not.”

In mining & construction, near-misses are discussed in morning tool talks —
but night-shift workers, absentees, or low-literacy teams often miss critical insights.
Oral communication fails. Translation distorts. Attention fades.
Result? The same incident repeats.

We fix this with verified, visual, and trackable safety delivery.

How Zero Harm Studio Closes the Loop

1. Incident → 3D Video

We convert your near-miss or incident into a clear, engaging 3D animation — showing exactly what happened and why.

2. Secure App Delivery

Each worker gets a unique login. Video opens only in our secure app — no forwarding, no skipping.

3. Real-Time Tracking

Admin dashboard shows: Who watched? How much? Which department is 100% compliant?

Key Features of Our Safety App

  • ✅ Unique ID + Password for every worker — no shared access
  • ✅ Video opens only inside the app — not on YouTube or WhatsApp
  • ✅ 100% viewing mandatory — partial views not counted
  • ✅ Department-wise analytics — see who’s compliant, who’s pending
  • ✅ Monthly Safety Rankings — motivate teams with visibility
  • ✅ Works on low-end smartphones — no high-end device needed
